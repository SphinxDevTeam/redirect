Farewell to Sphinx

It is with a heavy heart that we announce the closure of Sphinx. Since our launch in April, we've faced significant challenges in generating the necessary revenue to maintain our operations.

We are deeply grateful to all our users who have supported us throughout this journey. While this chapter ends, our mission continues with Rho-9, where we'll be focusing our expertise on enhancing Discord security.

We're excited to bring the same dedication and innovation to this new venture and hope you'll join us on this path forward.
